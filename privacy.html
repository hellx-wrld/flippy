Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your data when using Flippy Flip Bot.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Data

  • Discord User ID: To identify and track your bot usage
  • Server/Guild Information: Server ID, member count for analytics
  • Command Usage: Commands used, frequency, and timestamps
  • Game Statistics: Coins, levels, items, and game progress

User-Provided Data

  • Profile Customization: Theme preferences, custom colors
  • Premium Subscriptions: Payment information (processed by secure third parties)
  • Support Communications: Messages sent to our support team

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve bot functionality
  • Track game progress and statistics
  • Process premium subscriptions and features
  • Generate analytics and performance metrics
  • Provide customer support
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure databases, and regular security audits.
  • Database Security: MongoDB Atlas with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Access Control: Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Data Retention: User data retained for service provision, deleted upon account deletion
  • Backup Systems: Secure, encrypted backups for data protection

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We DO NOT sell your personal data. We may share data in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Consent: When you explicitly provide consent

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Data Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Data Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our dashboard uses minimal cookies for:

  • Theme preferences (dark/light mode)
  • Session management
  • Analytics (anonymous usage data)

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.
