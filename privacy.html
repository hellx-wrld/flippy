Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using Flippy Flip Bot. This policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations.

1. Information We Collect

Essential Data (Required for Service)

  • Discord User ID: Unique identifier to link your Discord account with our services
  • Discord Username: For display purposes and user identification
  • Server/Guild Information: Server ID and basic metadata for bot functionality
  • Command Interactions: Commands used and interaction timestamps for service delivery

Game & Profile Data

  • Virtual Economy: Coin balance, transaction history, and virtual item inventory
  • Game Statistics: Wins, losses, games played, experience points, and achievements
  • Profile Customization: Themes, colors, backgrounds, and display preferences
  • Subscription Data: Premium status, subscription tier, and billing information (via secure payment processors)

Technical & Analytics Data

  • Usage Analytics: Feature usage patterns, session duration, and error logs
  • Device Information: Basic browser/client information for dashboard access
  • Performance Data: Response times and system performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Service Delivery: Provide core bot functionality and maintain game state
  • User Experience: Personalize features, save preferences, and track progress
  • Premium Services: Process subscriptions and deliver enhanced features
  • Support: Troubleshoot issues and provide customer assistance

Secondary Uses

  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns to improve services
  • Security: Detect fraud, abuse, and maintain system integrity
  • Communications: Send important service updates and premium notifications
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and respond to legal requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Enterprise-Grade Security: We implement bank-level security measures to protect your data.

Security Measures

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Database Security: MongoDB Atlas with IP whitelisting and VPC networking
  • Access Control: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access for staff
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly security assessments and vulnerability scans
  • Backup Systems: Encrypted daily backups with 30-day retention

Data Retention

  • Active Users: Data retained while account is active and for 12 months after last activity
  • Deleted Accounts: Most data deleted within 30 days; some anonymized analytics may be retained
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We NEVER sell your personal data. We only share data in these limited, specific circumstances:

Authorized Sharing

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal for premium subscription processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Cloud Services: MongoDB Atlas, Discord API for essential service functionality
  • Analytics: Anonymized, aggregated data only (no personal identifiers)
  • Support Tools: Limited data for customer service platforms

Legal Requirements

  • Court orders, subpoenas, or other legal process
  • Preventing fraud or investigating security incidents
  • Protecting our rights, property, or safety
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service

5. Your Privacy Rights

Universal Rights (All Users)

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request complete account and data deletion
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications

Enhanced Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

  • Processing Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Automated Decision-Making: Opt-out of automated profiling
  • Supervisory Authority: File complaints with your local data protection authority

How to Exercise Rights: Contact us using the information below. We'll respond within 30 days and verify your identity before processing requests.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Dashboard Cookies

  • Essential: Session management, authentication, security (cannot be disabled)
  • Functional: Theme preferences, language settings, dashboard customization
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics, performance monitoring (can be opted out)

Discord Bot Data

The Discord bot does not use traditional cookies but collects interaction data as described in Section 1.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data
  • Privacy Shield successor frameworks where applicable
  • Adequacy decisions for approved countries
  • Additional safeguards for sensitive data

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Age Requirement: Our service is intended for users 13 and older.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If we discover collection from a minor, we will delete the information immediately
  • Parents can contact us to review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information
  • Teen users (13-17) have the same privacy rights as adults

9. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach:

  • We will investigate and contain the breach within 24 hours
  • Affected users will be notified within 72 hours
  • Relevant authorities will be notified as required by law
  • We will provide clear information about what happened and what we're doing about it

10. Privacy by Design

We implement privacy protection from the ground up:

  • Data Minimization: Collect only what's necessary for functionality
  • Purpose Limitation: Use data only for stated purposes
  • Storage Limitation: Retain data only as long as necessary
  • Transparency: Clear, understandable privacy practices
